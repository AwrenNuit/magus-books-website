Lost Teachings of the Runes invites the reader to journey to the realms of past and future that exist hidden beyond the horizon and beneath our feet. Using an engaging blend of stories, meditations, and ancestral knowing, author Ingrid Kincaid explores Northern Mysteries from the center of the Wheel of Life. Kincaid demonstrates ways the Wheel can be used to connect ancient wisdom with modern life, and offers tools and teachings that may be used on a daily basis to enable readers to reclaim their personal power. Lost Teachings of the Runes presents a life-affirming, death-honoring approach that returns the runes to a place of balance, to light and dark, to order and chaos, and to the roots and branches of the world tree.

Violet

Violet has been used in healing traditions throughout Eurasia. It has been lauded for an ability to lift the spirits, move gunky coughs, help the skin with various rashes and irritations, and so much more. A little in a cup of tea, perhaps a teaspoon, can be great for these and other internal issues like the cough, but it is far more popular in lotions and cremes for the skin.

Coriander Seed Oil

Getting used to Lavender EO? Familiar with favorite citrus and spice EOs? Then this is the perfect EO to add to any basic collection. It blends well with Eos like ginger, cinnamon and clove and these four Eos can be used in a diffuser to create a warming, heart opening feeling in the room it is released in. Coriander also blends well with citrus oils like lemongrass. These can be inhaled or rubbed on the abdomen after a really big meal that might make digestion uncomfortable.

Garnet

A stone of love, loyalty and legend, garnet is an ancient stone most often found in metamorphic rock – a sign that garnet can help anyone survive change. It also grounds people, generating energy and reconnecting us to the world – all of which means we can refocus on what is going on around us and get back to fulfilling our dreams for a new relationship, finishing that great art piece, or just hanging in for that next paycheck. All of the Mars and fire energies associated with garnet is thought to ignite will power and get us moving to reach goals and focus on the important stuff in life.

Shiva Nataraja

Invite the Lord of the Dance into your home! He dances in joy, exuberance, and exultation of Life, and he will steadily sway when storms of sorrow pass through. He is one of the most well known of the Hindu deities. Shiva's cosmic dance encapsulates creation, destruction; life, death; and all in between. It is the perfect time of year for you to join Shiva Nataraja in his dance as we enter into creating and celebrating a New Year!
