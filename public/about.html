About Us

Founded in 1992, Magus started out in a tiny little shop, and has expanded and evolved thru the years thanks to all the seekers who came to explore our store and enrich themselves - we look forward to seeing you all again and meeting new seekers every day.

Magus offers books on spirituality covering a multitude of paths, from Agnosticism to Zoroastrianism; we are a one stop shop for all your spiritual & wellness needs. Whether you are a church going Christian or a Voodoo Priestess, we offer a safe space for you to explore yourself and your relationship with the universe we all live in. We also offer traditional ritual tools, including Wands, Athames, Chalices, Singing Bowls, & precious gemstones. In addition, we have one have one of the largest selections of herbs and resins in North America including high grade frankincense and myrrh, as well as a variety of smudge sticks.

Everyday at Magus is an adventure! We hope you will join us on our exploration. We are here to be a Shining Light, to Expand Horizons!
